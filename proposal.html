Full Online Ordering & Checkout

Customers can build a complete order, hats, polos, hoodies, add it to a cart, enter their shipping info, and pay securely with a credit card. No back-and-forth DMs needed.

Artwork Upload

After checkout, customers upload their logo or design file directly on the site. No more chasing files over email or text. Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, PDF, and AI files.

Customer Accounts

Customers can create an account, log back in, and view their order history, saved artwork, and saved addresses. Makes repeat orders faster and keeps everything organized for them.

Admin Dashboard

You get a private admin panel to manage every order. See what was ordered, upload the digital proof for customer approval, update order status, and keep a full history. Everything in one place, no spreadsheets needed.

Automatic Email Notifications

Customers get an order confirmation the moment they check out. You get an alert for every new order. When you upload a proof, the customer gets notified automatically. All hands-free.

📦

Live Shipping Rates

Real shipping costs are calculated automatically based on the customer's location and order size. No guessing, no flat rates, just accurate pricing pulled in real time at checkout.

Works on Every Device

The entire site, from product browsing to checkout, is fully optimized for mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. Your customers can place an order from anywhere.
